# Features Research

**Domain:** Forestry Management & Offline Fleet Ops GIS
**Researched:** 2026-04-18
**Confidence:** HIGH

## Expected Features

### Table Stakes (Must Have)
- **Offline Progressive Web App:** Purely manual ingestion (no AI) of sapling height, canopy, stem diameter, and Alive/Dead status without connectivity.
- **Cryptographic Immutability:** GPS coordinates linked strictly to timestamped photos to enforce "ground truth."
- **Dashboard Clustering:** Visual canopy tracking that clusters tightly at macro-levels and explodes out down to the specific tree tag upon zooming.
- **Role-Based Access Control:** Separation between viewing (Citizens), administration (Dept Officials), and randomized verification tasks (Auditors).

### Competitive Differentiators
- **Dynamic QR URL Routing:** Real-world tree tags drive specific domain profiles for donations/audits immediately upon phone scan.
- **Stratified Audit Engine:** Algorithmic queuing telling auditors exactly which subset of a local cluster they must counter-verify.
- **Automated Certificate PDF Engine:** Triggered upon NGO assignment of donor logic.

### Anti-Features (Deliberately Avoid)
- **AI Plant Diagnostics Mobile Models:** Wasteful computation. Forces a UX hit in low-service environments, inflating data transmission sizes.
- **Complex Consumer Checkout Gateways:** Let NGOs handle Stripe consumer donations on their website; we strictly handle the SaaS portal and PDF generation.
